================================================================================
                         PAYROLL FLOW — PayRex Application
================================================================================

OVERVIEW
--------
The payroll process moves through four stages involving three roles:
  Employee  →  HR  →  Accountant  →  Admin

Each role has specific responsibilities that ensure accuracy, compliance,
and proper authorization before salaries are released.


================================================================================
STAGE 1 — EMPLOYEE (Daily Input)
================================================================================

Responsibility: Clock in/out, request leaves, review personal payslips.

  1. Clock In / Clock Out
     - Employee scans their QR code at the attendance terminal.
     - System validates that the QR code belongs to the employee's company.
     - Records TimeIn, TimeOut, LateMinutes, OvertimeHours, and Status.
     - Late minutes beyond the grace period (from Company Settings) are tracked.

  2. Leave Requests
     - Employee submits leave requests (Vacation, Sick, Emergency, etc.)
       via the Employee Portal.
     - Pending leaves await HR approval.

  3. View My Payslips
     - After payroll is released, employees can view their payslip details
       including earnings breakdown (Basic, OT, Holiday, Allowances) and
       deductions (SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, Withholding Tax).
     - Payslips can be exported to PDF.

  4. View My Attendance
     - Employee reviews their own attendance history and statistics.


================================================================================
STAGE 2 — HR (Data Preparation & Validation)
================================================================================

Responsibility: Manage employees, approve leaves, verify attendance.

  1. Employee Management
     - Add/edit employees: personal info, employment type, salary rate,
       position, department, and assigned roles.
     - Assign employee codes and generate QR codes.
     - Activate or deactivate employees.

  2. Leave Approval
     - Review pending leave requests.
     - Approve or reject with optional notes.
     - Approved leaves are reflected as "On Leave" in attendance records.

  3. Attendance Review
     - Monitor daily attendance logs.
     - Verify late arrivals, absences, and overtime entries.
     - Archive attendance records for historical reference.

  4. Compensation Setup
     - Add employee-specific deductions (loans, advances, etc.).
     - Add employee-specific benefits/allowances (rice, transport, etc.).
     - These are factored into payroll computation automatically.


================================================================================
STAGE 3 — ACCOUNTANT (Payroll Computation & Contributions)
================================================================================

Responsibility: Create payroll periods, compute salaries, manage contributions.

  1. Create Payroll Period
     - Define the pay period (start date → end date, period name).
     - Example: "Mar 01 - 15, 2026" for semi-monthly payroll.

  2. Compute Salaries  [POST /api/payroll/compute/{periodId}]
     - System automatically calculates for all active employees:

       a) Basic Pay
          - Uses Period Rate based on Payroll Frequency (Company Settings):
            * Semi-Monthly: Monthly Salary / 2
            * Monthly: Full Monthly Salary
          - Daily Rate = Period Rate / Working Days in Period
          - Basic Pay = Daily Rate × Days Present

       b) Overtime Pay
          - Hourly Rate = Daily Rate / Work Hours Per Day (Company Settings)
          - OT Pay = OT Hours × Hourly Rate × Overtime Multiplier (Company Settings)

       c) Holiday Pay
          - Holiday Pay = Daily Rate × Holiday Rate Multiplier (Company Settings)
            for each holiday day the employee was present.

       d) Allowances
          - Sum of all employee benefits/allowances.

       e) Gross Pay = Basic + Overtime + Holiday + Allowances

       f) Government Contributions (based on System Settings rates):
          - SSS: Gross Pay × SSS Rate
          - PhilHealth: Gross Pay × PhilHealth Rate
          - Pag-IBIG: Gross Pay × Pag-IBIG Rate (capped at PHP 200)

       g) Other Deductions
          - Employee-specific deductions (loans, advances, etc.)
          - Late deduction: minutes beyond grace period × per-minute rate

       h) Total Deductions = SSS + PhilHealth + Pag-IBIG + Other Deductions + Late

       i) Net Pay = Gross Pay - Total Deductions

  3. Review Summaries
     - Accountant reviews computed payroll summaries per employee.
     - Verify all amounts, deductions, and contributions are correct.

  4. Government Contributions Report
     - View SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG breakdowns per employee per period.
     - Both Employee Share and Employer Share are recorded.


================================================================================
STAGE 4 — ADMIN (Approval & Release)
================================================================================

Responsibility: Approve payroll, generate payslips, release to employees.

  1. Approve Payroll Period
     - Admin reviews the computed payroll summaries.
     - Changes period status from Draft → Approved.

  2. Generate Payslips  [POST /api/payroll/payslips/generate/{periodId}]
     - Creates individual payslip records for each employee in the period.
     - Each payslip includes: Basic Pay, Overtime, Holiday Pay, Allowances,
       Gross Pay, Total Deductions, and Net Pay.

  3. Release Payslips
     - Changes payroll period status to Released.
     - Employees can now view their payslips in the Employee Portal.

  4. Finance Tracking
     - Track income and expenses for the company.
     - Monitor overall payroll costs per period.


================================================================================
COMPANY SETTINGS (Configuration Reference)
================================================================================

These settings affect payroll computation:

  Setting                     | Default    | Effect
  ----------------------------+------------+--------------------------------------------
  Payroll Cycle (Frequency)   | Semi-Mthly | Determines how monthly salary is divided
  Work Hours Per Day          | 8.0        | Used for hourly/OT rate calculation
  Overtime Rate Multiplier    | 1.25×      | Applied to overtime hours
  Late Grace Minutes          | 15 min     | Minutes before late deduction kicks in
  Holiday Rate Multiplier     | 2.0×       | Applied to holiday present days
  Scheduled Time In           | 08:00      | Expected clock-in time
  Scheduled Time Out          | 17:00      | Auto-calculated from TimeIn + WorkHours + 1hr lunch
  Vacation Leave Days         | 15         | Annual/monthly leave allocation
  Vacation Leave Reset Type   | Yearly     | Monthly (0) or Yearly (1)

PAYROLL PERIOD STATUS LIFECYCLE
================================================================================

  Draft  ──►  Approved  ──►  Released
    │                           │
    │  (Accountant computes)    │  (Admin generates payslips)
    │                           │
    ▼                           ▼
  Can recompute              Employees can view payslips


================================================================================
API ENDPOINTS REFERENCE
================================================================================

  Payroll Periods:
    GET    /api/payroll/periods              List all periods
    POST   /api/payroll/periods              Create a new period

  Computation:
    POST   /api/payroll/compute/{periodId}   Compute salaries for a period

  Summaries:
    GET    /api/payroll/summaries/{periodId} Get computed summaries

  Contributions:
    GET    /api/payroll/contributions        Get government contributions

  Compensation:
    GET    /api/payroll/compensation         Get deductions & benefits
    POST   /api/payroll/deductions           Add employee deduction
    POST   /api/payroll/benefits             Add employee benefit

  Payslips:
    GET    /api/payroll/payslips             Get payslips for current user
    POST   /api/payroll/payslips/generate/{periodId}  Generate payslips
